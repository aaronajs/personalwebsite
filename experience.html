Co-Founder, Developer and Designer

Leadership Strategic Planning Creative Thinking

Along with a school colleague, I started a business that aimed to provide a new digital and hybrid format of a textbook and revision guide app for GCSE students for mathematics and the three sciences. Our plan was to begin with these subjects then expand to a wider variety.

We aimed to create a textbook that could be accessed by students on any device. The textbook would be refined to omit common errors seen in most books today and take advantage of technology that was already available to consumers. From utilising Apple’s “Handoff” features to ensure fluid revision between multiple devices to using augmented and virtual reality headsets to provide detailed three-dimensional diagrams.

I created the website for our company and carefully designed the textbooks, diagrams and official media for use in advertising. I helped to create our online presence by using both search engine optimisation and through social media. I gained experience with Amazon Web Services, Adobe Photoshop and Pixelmator through this. I also presented to large groups of people at schools to talk about our product and conducted public surveys across Manchester City Centre.
